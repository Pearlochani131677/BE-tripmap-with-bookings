# 🌍 TripMap – AI-Powered Travel Planning System

TripMap is an intelligent travel planning platform designed to solve real-world group travel challenges. It combines rule-based logic with lightweight AI models to generate safe, personalized, and budget-aware itineraries, along with travel and hotel suggestions.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🧠 Smart Itinerary Generation
- Multi-factor scoring (fun, comfort, safety)
- Personalized plans based on traveler profiles
- Dynamic adjustment based on budget and duration

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Group Travel Optimization
- Resolves conflicting preferences among travelers
- Balances adventure vs relaxation activities
- Ensures maximum group satisfaction

### 🏥 Medical & Child Safety Filter
- Removes risky activities for:
  - Children
  - Elderly travelers
  - Medical conditions (asthma, heart issues, etc.)
- Suggests safer alternatives automatically

### 💰 Budget Intelligence
- Predicts realistic trip costs
- Avoids unrealistic planning scenarios
- Distributes budget across itinerary

### 🤖 AI Travel Chatbot
- Hybrid system (TinyLLaMA + rule-based logic)
- Supports:
  - Travel queries
  - Weather advice
  - Safety tips
  - Budget guidance
- Bilingual support (English + Hindi)

### ✈️ Travel & 🏨 Hotel Suggestions
- Provides options for:
  - Flights, trains, buses, car travel
- Hotel recommendations based on:
  - Budget
  - Number of days
- Helps users make informed booking decisions

- SQLite
